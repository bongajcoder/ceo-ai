## 🚧 RISKS & MITIGATION STRATEGIES

### Potential Roadblocks

**Technical Risks:**
- AI hallucinations leading to bad decisions
  - **Mitigation:** Human approval for critical decisions, confidence scores, audit trail

- Scaling challenges as userbase grows
  - **Mitigation:** Cloud-native architecture, horizontal scaling, load testing

- Data breaches or security vulnerabilities
  - **Mitigation:** Regular security audits, penetration testing, SOC 2 compliance

**Business Risks:**
- Users don't adopt AI recommendations
  - **Mitigation:** Gradual trust-building, prove value early, transparency in reasoning

- Competition from established players
  - **Mitigation:** Focus on liberation philosophy differentiation, community lock-in

- Economic downturn affecting creative industries
  - **Mitigation:** Diversified revenue streams, creator-first economics, essential tool positioning

**Organizational Risks:**
- Bongani's time constraints limiting feedback
  - **Mitigation:** Hire executive assistant, automate feedback collection, use AI to interpret usage data

- Key team member departure
  - **Mitigation:** Documentation, knowledge sharing, overlap in skills, competitive comp
